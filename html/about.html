About Me

Who I am and what I do.
Josh Neuman downhill skateboarding -- one of my hobbies

Born to two California-native parents, I have come to love the state I live in. I come from a family of five where our lives revolve around the ocean and Major League Baseball which has had a profound impact on my upbringing.

I enjoy everything outside and inside which doesn't necessarily make me very unique, but I can find enjoyment in anything. From surfing, hiking, running and kite-flying to reading, drawing, cooking and videogames, I spend hours a week learning how to enhance my horizons.

I graduated from Dana Hills High School in 2019 before heading on a full-time two year volunteer mission trip for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints to Lima, Peru. Due to Covid-19, I came home about a year early and finished my service in Milwaukee, Wisconsin before coming home. I currently attend Brigham Young University as a Pre-Business Major with hopes of graduating in Information Systems.
